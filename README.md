# Portfolio Stress Testing Project
Portfolio stress testing and risk analysis during market crisis periods.

## Objective
Analyze portfolio behavior during market crises.

## Assets
SPY, TLT, GLD, etc.

## Methods
- Historical Simulation
- Maximum Drawdown
- Value at Risk

## Key Findings
- SPY experienced severe drawdowns during the COVID-19 market crash.
- TLT showed unexpectedly large losses during the high inflation and rate hiking regime.
- GLD provided partial downside protection during market stress periods.
